# 08 — Systems

Optional and integrated **building systems** beyond basic mechanical — many earthship-adjacent.

## Scope

- Rainwater collection (roof area, storage, tie to tanks or separate cistern)
- Greywater reuse (legal constraints vary widely)
- Blackwater / septic vs. sewer
- Solar PV (roof orientation — north-sloping rear roof may be primary array surface)
- Battery storage / grid tie
- Domestic hot water (solar thermal, heat pump, tank preheat)
- Composting toilet (if pursuing off-grid or water reduction)
