**Status:** Not started

## Question

Can ~3,360 gallons of interior water behind south glass meaningfully regulate temperature in a 1,500 sq ft insulated building in the target climate?

## Constraints

- Tanks: 2 × (18" × 15' × 10') along 30' of 60' south wall
- Envelope is insulation-forward, not mass walls
- Tanks impose ~28,000 lb load and leak/maintenance risk
- Glass zone creates gain and nighttime loss

## Options

1. Tanks as primary mass only (passive)
2. Tanks + hydronic coupling to heating system
3. Reduce tank volume / count
4. Substitute partial mass (concrete bench, phase-change materials)
