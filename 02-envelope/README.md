# 02 — Envelope

The **insulated shell**: walls, roof, floor, windows (thermal properties), air sealing, vapor control.

## Scope

- Wall assembly (framing, insulation type, R-target)
- Roof assembly (slope, insulation, venting or unvented)
- Floor / slab insulation
- Window U-factor and SHGC targets (coordinate with 03-passive-solar)
- Air tightness goal (e.g. ACH50)
- Moisture management for climate

## Design intent

**Insulation standards, not thermal mass walls.** The envelope should minimize heat loss/gain; thermal inertia comes from water tanks (04), not from walls.
